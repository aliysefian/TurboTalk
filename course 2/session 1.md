# ✅ **1. I'm feeling great**

**Persian Translation:** من احساس خیلی خوبی دارم  
**Part of Speech:** Expression / Sentence  
**Meaning:** Used to say you feel very good physically or emotionally.  
**Usage:** Common in daily conversation when answering “How are you?”  
**Synonyms:** I feel awesome, I feel very good, I’m doing great.  
**Pronunciation:** /aɪm ˈfiːlɪŋ ɡreɪt/  
**Common Collocations:** feeling great today, feeling great now  
**Cultural Note:** A positive and friendly English response.  
**Example:** "I'm feeling great after my morning walk."
